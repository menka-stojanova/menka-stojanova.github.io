Let's make Pangea

This workshops was first conducted at the Natural History Museum in Valparaiso, Chile. Participants used cut-out continents and data about fossils to recreate Pangea. We discussed the development of theory of plate tectonics, from A. Wegener to M. Tharp to modern days.

Science Workshops and Dissemination

While working as a scientist I participated in dissemination events organised with my colleagues. In 2018 I spent a few months creating and animating my own science workshops for children, in Valparaiso, Chile. Funded in part by University Adolfo Ibáñez and organised in collaboration with museums, cultural venues and schools, these workshops consisted on hands-on activities for children and multi-level explanations that get children and adults engaged.
